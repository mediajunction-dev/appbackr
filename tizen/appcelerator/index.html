What is Appcelerator?

What is ?

Tizen will help HTML5 developers monetize their apps.

Tizen is an open-source and standard-based software and based on the Linux operating system. It's backed by hardware makers that are building devices running the Tizen Platform. These devices need apps.

FOR THE USER

Tizen is an open ecosystem and compatible with all browsers. It offers a consistent experience across all devices for the user

FOR THE DEVELOPER

Tizen provides developers a new operating system to source and distribute their apps.

What is an appscore?

appbackr appscore gives every app a score from 1.0 - 10.0.

The appscore uses a machine-learning model, evolving and adapting to score apps on their value. Apps that score high, receive cash incentive to deploy to Tizen.

FOR THE DEVELOPER

appbackr appscore gets the right apps featured where consumers browse for apps. appscore helps developers get distribution.

FOR THE STORE

App stores rely on the appscore to curate the best apps for their customers. They source and feature those apps to maximize store revenue.
